Advanced Calculus

MATH 201 • Section 03
4 Credits

description Course Description

This advanced course covers limits, continuity, differentiation, and integration of functions of a single variable. Students will master fundamental theorems and apply integration techniques to solve complex problems. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical understanding and practical application in engineering and physical sciences contexts.

school Learning Objectives

  • Understand and apply the rigorous definitions of limits and continuity.
  • Master differentiation techniques for transcendental functions.
  • Apply integration to calculate areas, volumes, and arc lengths.
  • Analyze infinite sequences and series for convergence.
